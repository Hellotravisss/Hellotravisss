**Mon–Fri** — I work the floor at a brick & paver plant in Metro Vancouver. Real bricks, real kilns, real forklifts.

**Behind the lens** — 21 years across 33 countries: architecture, interiors and hospitality for architects, designers and hotels across BC. Former contributing photographer for *Harper's Bazaar* (2012–2023); cinematographer on Sundaram Tagore's Louis Kahn documentary *Tiger City*. Bilingual EN / 中文.

**After hours** — I design, build and deploy my own tools, solo, with AI as a working partner the whole way. It started with an app for my own plant floor.
